DOMINIQUE MARCIL

Hello, I'm Dominique Marcil, a passionate Junior Front End Developer dedicated to crafting inclusive and accessible user experiences. My expertise lies in ensuring that every interaction is intuitive, diverse, and equitable for all users.

My work is driven by a commitment to diversity and accessibility, where I strive to create websites that offer multiple navigation options, leveraging APIs for visual tracking to accommodate users who may not rely on traditional mouse navigation. Additionally, I specialize in implementing thoughtful design choices such as customizable color schemes and font options to enhance usability for individuals with varying levels of visual abilities.

With a focus on user-centric design principles, I continuously seek to bridge the gap between technology and inclusivity, making the web a more welcoming and accommodating space for everyone.
